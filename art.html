ART

VISUAL ART

A Visual artist in an entertainment context is the one responsible for throwing visuals on the performer(a DJ or a Live musician) using a projector and custom software. The dynamics differ if the wall behind is instead an LCD screen. The quality of the colour decreases and the brightness of the visuals is REALLY HIGH. Projecting images and videos can be in a traditional form(Storytelling approach) or be more complicated as in the case of Projection Mapping (projecting on 3D surfaces).
