I'm a web developer with about 2 years experience, currently working with Cognizant Technology solutions. I'm good at HTML5, CSS, Javascript, UX/UI design, Responsive design, Requirements engineering, Software engineering, and User-centered, data driven Design. I hold a Masters in Software Engineering from VIT University

Shivacult is my music project.. I make ambient music with STRONG classical Carnatic music influence that is Aesthetic, Trippy and is suitable for spiritual practises such as Yoga and Tai-chi. I feel, experimental installations with music/dance/art happening LIVE is the way to go!

I'm a DJ/Producer/Visual artist, currently involved in making Boutique music under the monicker "Shivacult". My music is exquisitely engineered and aesthetically constructed. No, I really mean it! I follow the Carnatic approach to music albeit in a very sonically sophisticated form. I'm also a trained Carnatic vocalist.

I take some time off sometimes to put my thoughts into paper. Although I'm not trained in this field formally, I'm trying to educate myself with the nuances of writing and the ethics of communication. To report something as-is, without putting in your own ideas into it is indeed a Huge task!
